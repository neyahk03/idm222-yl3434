drexel academic website

Project overview

Drexel students face difficulty scheduling tutoring appointments through the Academic Resource Center's website due to complex and unfriendly user interfaces. Their goal is to access tutoring services seamlessly. A user friendly interface can make accessing tutors a seamless process, which in turn can increase the chance of a student successfully scheduling and improving their academic career.

My role: Interaction design, Prototyping, Usability Testing, Visual Design, UX designer
