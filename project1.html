Project overview

This project offers a comprehensive exploration of functional, standards-based content creation for the internet, encompassing design, delivery, and maintenance. Participants will blend aesthetics with markup languages, critically assessing web design quality, and mastering website creation and upkeep. Additionally, we will delve into accessibility and web design standards, understanding their significance. The project will delve into the principles and techniques of modern website construction, utilizing HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Practical application will be emphasized through hands-on exercises and real-world projects.

My role: Interaction design, Prototyping, Usability Testing, Visual Design.

Tools: Figma, HTML, CSS, Javascript
