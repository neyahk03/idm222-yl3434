Zodiac Generator

Project overview

In this project, we're tasked with crafting a web-based application using the provided zodiac algorithm, marking the culmination of our course journey. Our goal is to create an interactive platform where users can input their birth dates to discover their western zodiac signs, with the option to easily recalculate for different dates. What sets our project apart is the immersive experience we aim to deliver: each zodiac sign will be visually represented uniquely, accompanied by distinct sound effects, making the exploration of astrology both engaging and captivating. Additionally, we're incorporating a helpful guide within the app to ensure users can navigate and enjoy its features seamlessly.

My role: Interaction design, Prototyping, Visual Design.

Tools: Figma, HTML, CSS, Javascript

Live link:zodiac-yenluong.vercel.app
